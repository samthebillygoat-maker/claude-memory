## Notes / Ideas

- ChatGPT image gen outputs at 1024x1536 (vertical 2:3) or 1122x1402 (4:5). For posters 16x20+, ALWAYS upscale to 300 DPI before sending to a printer — Walgreens/Walmart will reject for resolution otherwise.
- Per-piece resolution math: print_width_inches × 300 DPI = pixels needed wide. 24x36 = 7200x10800. 16x20 = 4800x6000. 11x14 = 3300x4200.
- Walgreens Posters builder is one-poster-at-a-time, separate SKU per size. Annoying but workable.
