## Key Discovery: The +1792 Fake Number Pattern

**This keeps appearing — learn to spot it fast:**
- Area code 792 does not exist in the North American Numbering Plan
- Any bulk list with lots of +1792 numbers = trash, skip entirely
- Source: Craigslist spam farms embed these in listing text
- Fix: Added `792` to the blocked area codes in `batch_national_scrape.py`
