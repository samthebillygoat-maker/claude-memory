# 🚢 Customs Business

---

## What It Is

Customs brokerage and consulting business. Helping clients import goods correctly — HTS codes, admissibility, partner agency requirements, duties.

---

## Current Challenges

- AI gives incorrect/outdated customs info — regulations change too fast
- Hard to track all partner agency updates (CBP, FDA, USDA, NOAA, FWS)
- Need to hire — screening resumes manually takes too long

## Key Regulatory Areas to Watch

- CBP CAPE system (IEEPA refunds) — clients need to file before July 24 sunset risk
- Section 122 15% surcharge — court challenge active, could be struck down
- Section 232 metals restructured Apr 6, 2026 — full customs value now applies
- NOAA MMPA seafood prohibitions — tuna, mahi-mahi most exposed
- FDA enforcement sweep Apr 2026 — food products, seafood, herbs
- De minimis fully suspended — all low-value shipments now pay duties
- Forced Labor Portal mandatory — all reviews through cbp.gov/trade/forced-labor
