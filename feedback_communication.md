name: Communication preferences
description: How this user likes to be communicated with
type: feedback
---

Keep responses short and direct — user communicates in brief messages and expects brief answers.

**Why:** User is action-oriented and doesn't want to read paragraphs. They ask short questions and want short answers.

**How to apply:** Lead with the answer or action. No preamble. If something needs explanation, bullet points over paragraphs. Skip summaries at the end of responses.
