## Why the reshuffle worked

- **P5 (crescent moon) + P6 (sun)** were *designed* as a mirrored diptych — flanking P1 makes them point inward at the hero, much stronger than the original P2/P3 flanks.
- **P3 (two vases) + P7 (quiet arches)** both at 16×20 — accidentally matched into a clean bedroom pair when P7 printed wrong.
- **P2 (mis-sized at 11×14)** is the right physical size for above the toilet — moved to bathroom (its small scale becomes a feature, not a bug).

## Hanging specs

### Sofa wall (already hung — keep for unit 2)
- **P1 hero** centered between sofa-arm-end and TV-wall corner (NOT corner-to-corner — couch arm blocks corner-anchored centering).
- **P1 nail height: 72"** off floor (frame center at 57").
- **P5 nail:** 23" LEFT of P1's vertical center line, **64"** off floor.
- **P6 nail:** 23" RIGHT of P1's vertical center line, **64"** off floor.
- All three frame-centers on the same 57" horizontal line — stepped silhouette, 8" inset top and bottom on the flanks.
- 3" horizontal gap between each frame.

### Dining wall (pending)
- **P4 (16×20 vert.)** centered horizontally on the *dining table's midpoint* (not the wall's midpoint — table is the visual anchor).
- **Nail at 64" off floor** (frame center at 57").
- Watch the rattan flush-mount light: P4 top edge should clear it by ≥6" (no issue at 8 ft ceiling).

### Bedroom diptych (pending)
- **P3 LEFT, P7 RIGHT** (P3 is the heavier piece — reads more balanced on the left).
- **2" gap** between them. Combined footprint: 34" wide.
- Centered on the queen bed (60" wide) → pair starts **13"** in from each headboard end.
- **Bottom edge 8" above headboard top.**
- **Nail measurement: 26" straight up from the top of the headboard** (8" gap + 17" up the frame to keyhole). Same nail height for both pieces.

### Bathroom (pending Walmart frame)
- **P2 (11×14 print)** centered above the toilet.
- Buy 11×14 oak frame at Walmart Westshore tomorrow (3-pack ~$28 — 1 spare).
- Bottom edge **8–10" above the toilet tank**.
- Frame center at ~57" off floor if wall allows.

## Universal rules used

- Standard gallery eye-level: **frame center at 57" off floor** for any non-furniture wall.
- For above-furniture pieces: bottom of frame 8–10" above the back/top of the furniture.
- Different-sized frames in a row: align by **centers**, not tops/bottoms. Creates a stepped silhouette that reads as one coordinated row.
- Hardware: IKEA RÖDALM has built-in keyhole hanger ~3" from frame top — no D-rings or wire needed. Single drywall nail per frame is enough (6 lb 6 oz max weight).
