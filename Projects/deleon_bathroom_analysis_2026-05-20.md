Professional reglazing, Hillsborough County avg $418–763. No structural change, reversible.

- Revenue lift: +$200–250/mo

### Option 3 — Tile Extension to Ceiling: $68–104 DIY / $270–370 with labor
Extend existing subway tile from current line to ceiling (~25–30 sqft).
- **DIY materials:** white 3x6 subway tile ($20–45), thinset ($15–20), grout ($12–18), spacers/tools ($20) = **~$68–104**
- **Hired labor:** $8–15/sqft Tampa rate + materials = **$270–370**
- Ask Erik first — frame as: "I'll cover cost, you keep the improvement"
- Most landlords say yes — it's a free upgrade to their unit
- DIY time: 4–6 hours over 2 days (thinset cures 24hrs before grouting)
- **Hardest part:** matching existing grout color — bring a photo to match in-store

### Option 4 — Light Refresh: $900–1,600 (ask Erik to allow)
- Paint walls above tile light gray
- New matte black vanity light fixture
- Matte black hardware throughout
- Revenue lift: +$300–350/mo

### Option 5 — Full Renovation: $8,000–18,000
**NOT SAM'S PROBLEM.** Tampa avg mid-range 5x7 bath: $9,979 (SPDC Jan 2026).
