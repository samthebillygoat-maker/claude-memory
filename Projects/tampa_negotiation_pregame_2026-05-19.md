# Tampa Negotiation Pre-Game

**Method:** Target rent = 50% of expected gross monthly revenue (Double Rent Rule).
**Gross revenue model:** STR comp × 0.70 (conservative 30-day corporate housing factor).
**Ackerman ladder:** Open 65% → C1 85% → C2 95% → Final 100% (non-round) + 1 concession.
**Walk-away line:** *"I respect that — it just doesn't work at that number for me. If anything changes, I'd love to revisit it."*
