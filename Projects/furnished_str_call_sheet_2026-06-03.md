## Double Rent Rule
Ratio = est. full-year-avg STR/MTR gross ÷ rent. **Miami** = true nightly STR (Sunny Isles / Aventura — verify each building's min-lease rule; many towers are 3–6mo seasonal). **Tampa** = 30+day mid-term (city 7-night min); furnished rents run high vs MTR revenue, so most are negotiate-down MAYBEs — lower-rent units pencil best. Reality check: the AirDNA Rentalizer number on Miami #1 came in ~$50.6K/yr — **well under** my market estimate — so treat estimated ratios as optimistic and verify the exact address in Rentalizer (comps matched to bed/bath) once a landlord is warm.
