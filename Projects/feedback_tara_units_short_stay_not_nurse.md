The Tara House West units (Hostaway 558584 / 558585) are **standard short-stay Airbnbs** — leisure + short visits. Sam explicitly rejected guest-facing listing copy that positioned them as "built for traveling nurses, relocations, and monthly stays."

**Why:** I (and the RankPilot/STR audit agent) kept defaulting to a traveling-nurse / 30+ night framing and crammed "monthly" + "nurse" keywords into the Airbnb listing copy. That's wrong for these units and attracts the wrong guest. The nurse / "30+ night corporate housing" language belongs to the **landlord-facing disclosure pitch** (see [[feedback_str_airbnb_disclosure]] / honest-disclosure) — it is NOT the guest-facing Airbnb positioning. Keep the two separate.

**How to apply:** Guest-facing Airbnb copy = short-stay, leisure, honest location facts. Keep genuine amenity/location keywords that are TRUE for short stays (full kitchen, workspace/desk, free parking, walkable to HCA South Tampa Hospital, minutes to downtown/SoHo/Bayshore). Do NOT add "traveling nurse," "relocation," "corporate housing," or front-load "monthly." See [[feedback_reality_check_dont_guess]].
