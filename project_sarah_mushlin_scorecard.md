## Current offer (3 service buckets on her site)
1. Design / test / refine programs in real classrooms
2. Implementation readiness & capacity building
3. Adoption, growth, scaling, fractional leadership
- No pricing public. Custom consulting.

## Product picked: District/Edtech Readiness Scorecard
**Free 10-min quiz → branded PDF result → upsell to $2,500 "Readiness Roadmap" call + memo → upsell again to $20–60k engagement.**

### Why this beats the other 4 ideas
- Cohort = trades time for money, no asset
- Pilot-in-a-Box / Funder-Ready Builder = 6–12 months to revenue, real SaaS competition
- AI advisor = thin moat
- **Scorecard** = free top of funnel, two ways to monetize (services + B2B SaaS), her name = instant credibility, MVP in a weekend (Tally + Zapier + PDF)

## 7-day build plan
- **Day 1**: One-sentence promise; pick ICP (recommend edtech founders); decide $2,500 Roadmap as paid offer
- **Day 2**: 30–40 questions across 5 dimensions (Leadership, Site capacity, Evidence/data, Equity, Growth model); 3 result tiers (Ready / Almost / Not Yet) with diagnosis + 3 actions each
- **Day 3**: Build in Tally (free) → Zapier → PDF via DocuPilot/PDFMonkey (~$25/mo) → Calendly CTA
- **Day 4**: Roadmap offer page + Stripe link + email sequence (results → case study → call nudge)
- **Day 5**: Personal DM to 30 warm contacts asking for feedback (target 15 takes)
- **Day 6–7**: Iterate, pull testimonials, record 90-sec Loom

## 30-day sales targets
- 100 quiz takes, 15 calls, 3 Roadmaps × $2,500 = **$7,500**
- 1 distribution partner (foundation or edtech newsletter)

## Year-1 revenue model

### Funnel math (industry-standard rates)
- Impression → quiz click: 1–3% (LinkedIn lead-magnet baseline)
- Start → finish: 40–60% (Typeform benchmark)
- Finish → call: 5–15%
- Call → close at $2,500: 20–40% (B2B services, warm + low-ticket)

### Scenarios
| Scenario | Roadmaps | Upsells | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 20 × $2.5k = $50k | 1–2 × $15–25k = $15–40k | **$65–90k** |
| Realistic | 40 × $2.5k = $100k | 4–6 × $20–40k = $80–240k | **$180–250k** |
| Strong | 60+ × $2.5k = $150k | 8–12 × $30–60k = $240–500k + licensing | **$450–700k** |

**Realistic target year 1: $150–250k.** Year 2 with proof + 1 partnership: $300–500k.

### Why upsells are the real revenue
$2,500 Roadmap is a **qualification fee**, not a product. Industry standard: 15–25% of paid-diagnostic clients convert to larger engagements. McKinsey/BCG/boutique playbook.

## Hours/week

## Verification questions (before building)
Ask Sarah:
1. In the last year, how many people asked for help when you didn't have a productized way to say yes? (Target: >20)
