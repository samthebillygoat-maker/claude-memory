## Tampa — zoning reframe

STR rules are **zone-by-zone, not citywide.** CG / CMU / CN allow STR; RS-50 / RS-60 do not. **FS 509.032 preempts local STR bans at the state level** — the killer is HOA covenants on specific buildings, not the city.

**Why this matters:** Sam's buddy operates in commercial / mixed-use zones (Channel District, Downtown, Ybor) using SFHs and small-landlord duplexes. Stop treating "Tampa" as a yes/no.

**How to apply:** for any Tampa lead, look up the parcel zoning before scoring. Bayshore SFHs are usually RS-zoned and should be filtered out unless the building is exempt.
