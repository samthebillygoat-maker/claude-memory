## The shared core paragraph (re-used in every email)

> CitySide Stays LLC is a Florida-based furnished rental operator. We sign **12-month master leases** on furnished units and host a mix of guests — leisure and business travelers, traveling nurses and project managers on 30+ night contracts, and relocating professionals. Stays range from a few nights to several months. We list on **Airbnb, VRBO, Furnished Finder**, and direct booking channels with the landlord's written consent in our **lease addendum** (attached).

## The shared "why this is safe for you" stack

- **Rent on the 1st**, every month, via ACH — regardless of occupancy. Vacancy risk lives with us.
- **First, last, and security deposit pre-paid** at signing.
- **CitySide Stays LLC is the sole tenant of record** on a 12-month master lease. Guests occupy as licensees, not sub-tenants.
- **All STR permits, business licenses, and lodging taxes are our responsibility** — addendum makes that explicit and includes a full indemnity to the owner.
- **$1M general liability + Airbnb AirCover.** Owner added as additional insured on request, COI within 5 business days.
- **Adaptive termination clause** — if local STR rules change mid-lease, we adapt the use to comply *or* exit cleanly with 30 days' notice and no penalty to the owner.
- **Guest screening:** government-ID verification, platform background screening where available, signed guest agreement (no parties, no smoking, no unauthorized pets).
- **On-site monitoring:** privacy-compliant **decibel-only** noise sensors (Minut / NoiseAware — no audio recording) + **outdoor-only video doorbell** at the entry. Never any cameras inside the unit.
- **All utilities, internet, routine maintenance, and turnover cleaning covered by us.**
