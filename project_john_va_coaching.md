## SCRIPT UPDATE — IMPORTANT (2026-05-08, evening)

The "corporate housing / 30+ night stays / traveling professionals only" framing is **out**. Sam's actual operation is mixed — short-term + mid-term + corporate stays, listed on Airbnb / VRBO / Furnished Finder with explicit landlord consent in the addendum. Saying "30+ nights only" misrepresents reality and creates legal exposure.

**The new opener — memorize verbatim:**

> "Hi, this is John from CitySide Stays. I saw your listing at [ADDRESS] — quick question, is it still available? *(if yes)* Great. I work with a small team that signs 12-month master leases on furnished units and hosts vetted travelers — a mix of leisure, business, and 30+ night corporate stays. We pay full rent on the 1st regardless of occupancy, we cover utilities, and we handle all the STR permits and taxes ourselves. **Is the owner open to a long-term tenant who lists on Airbnb under a signed addendum?**"

That last question is the new qualifier. If the answer is "no Airbnb," thank them and move on — don't argue.
