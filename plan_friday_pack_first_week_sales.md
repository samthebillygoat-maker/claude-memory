# Friday Pack — First-Week Sales Playbook

Created 2026-05-13. Product is LIVE at https://www.thefridaypack.com with promo "First 10 Weekly subscribers get a free Starter pack."

## Goal
3 paying customers in 7 days from organic + 1 cheap paid test.

## Priority order

### 1. Cold DM 30 STR arbitrage operators (highest ROI)
- Source: scrape Airbnb hosts in target markets showing arbitrage signals — multi-listing, generic photos, business name, "Stays" / "Suites" / "Properties" in name
- Markets to target first: Gulf Shores AL, Branson MO, Chattanooga TN (matches Friday Pack lead criteria)
- Message: short, personal, lead with the free Starter pack offer
- Goal: 3 paying customers from 30 DMs (10% close rate target)
- Channel: Instagram DM (where they post listing content) or LinkedIn if business name resolves

### 2. Reddit value posts (compounding)
- Subs: r/AirBnB, r/realestateinvesting, r/realestateinvesting
- DO NOT pitch the product. Post 2 genuinely useful pieces:
  - "I scraped 1,200 Tampa landlords last month — here's what the STR-friendly ones have in common"
  - "The 4-question filter I use to qualify STR arbitrage leads in under 60 seconds"
- Put `thefridaypack.com` in profile + first comment if mod allows
- Cross-post one to BiggerPockets STR forum

### 3. Twitter build-in-public (7-day cadence)
- One post per day for 7 days
- Audience orbit: Jeremy Werden, Avery Carl, Sieva Kozinsky, STR community
- Content mix:
  - 2 posts: anonymized real Tampa lead snippets ("called this PM today, they didn't even know what 'arbitrage' meant")
  - 2 posts: building-in-public stats (open rates, # leads delivered, # markets)
  - 2 posts: lessons from cold-calling landlords (Voss/Ackerman moments)
  - 1 post: the offer + screenshot of a Friday Pack
- End every post with a soft CTA to thefridaypack.com

### 4. Find 1 partner with an email list
- Target: STR coach / influencer with a list of 1k-10k STR operators
- Offer: 4 weeks of free Friday Packs for them in exchange for one dedicated email to their list
- People to ping: anyone selling an STR arbitrage course, anyone running an STR mastermind, anyone with a paid Discord
- Goal: one signed partnership by end of week

### 5. Google search ads (last priority)
- Bid on: "rental arbitrage leads", "STR leads", "Airbnb arbitrage rental list", "section 8 friendly landlords"
- Send to: `/samples/tampa` (existing live page that converts)
- Budget: $20/day to start
- Kill switch: CPL > $40 = pause and reassess

## Anti-patterns to avoid (from Friday Pack launch lessons)
- Don't claim "owner-direct" or "skip-traced" — call them "decision-maker phones" or "STR-friendly leads"
- Don't fabricate testimonials or numbers
- Don't spam Reddit — value first, link in profile

## Measurement
End-of-week dashboard:
- DMs sent / DMs replied / DMs closed
- Reddit upvotes + profile clicks
- Twitter impressions + thefridaypack.com clicks
- Partner emails sent / partners signed
- Google ads CPL + conversions
- New Stripe weekly subs
- New Stripe one-off packs

## Promo to remember
"First 10 Weekly subscribers get a free Starter pack delivered same week." Track manually in Stripe Dashboard — remove the bar when 10 weekly subs land (state: `state_friday_pack_active_promo.md`).
