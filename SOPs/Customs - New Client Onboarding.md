# 🚢 SOP — New Client Onboarding

---

## Step 1 — Initial Intake Call

- [ ] Get company name, contact name, phone, email
- [ ] Understand what they import (commodities, countries of origin)
- [ ] Get estimated import volume (entries per month, annual value)
- [ ] Identify which agencies apply (CBP, FDA, USDA, NOAA, FWS?)
- [ ] Discuss pain points — why are they switching brokers or starting fresh?
- [ ] Explain your services and pricing
- [ ] Send engagement letter / service agreement

---

## Step 2 — Document Collection

- [ ] IRS EIN / Tax ID
- [ ] Business license
- [ ] POA (Power of Attorney) — CBP Form 5291
- [ ] Importer of Record number (or apply for one)
- [ ] ACE Portal access setup (if not already registered)
- [ ] ACH refund authorization (for CAPE refunds — separate from duty payment ACH)
- [ ] HTS codes for their main commodities
- [ ] Supplier information (name, address, country)
- [ ] Prior broker records (if switching)

---

## Step 3 — Compliance Check

- [ ] Run commodities against current import alerts (FDA IA, CBP WROs)
- [ ] Check country of origin against active tariff regimes:
  - Section 122 (15% — expires Jul 24, 2026, court challenge active)
  - Section 232 (metals, semis, pharma, autos)
  - Section 301 (China + 15 other economies)
  - UFLPA / forced labor risk
- [ ] Check NOAA MMPA status if seafood importer
- [ ] Flag de minimis impact (fully suspended — all shipments pay full duties)
- [ ] Confirm ACE Portal enrollment and ACH refund setup

---

## Step 4 — Setup

- [ ] Add client to your system
- [ ] Create client folder (see [[Clients/]] folder)
- [ ] Set up entry filing access
- [ ] Brief client on current regulatory environment
- [ ] Schedule first entry / first shipment coordination call

---

## Key Warnings to Give Every New Client

- De minimis fully suspended — no $800 threshold relief
- IEEPA refunds available via CAPE portal (ACH refund account required separately)
- Section 122 tariff is 15% not 10%, and may be struck down by courts
- CBP refunds now ACH only — must enroll bank account
- Forced Labor Portal mandatory for any detained shipment reviews
