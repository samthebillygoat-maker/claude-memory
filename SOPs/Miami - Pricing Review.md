# 💲 SOP — Pricing Review

**Frequency:** Weekly (every Monday)

---

## Weekly Pricing Check

- [ ] Open PriceLabs or Wheelhouse dashboard
- [ ] Review next 30 days — any gaps in bookings?
- [ ] Check competitor rates in your neighborhood (Airbnb search, filter by 1BR)
- [ ] Adjust base rate if needed
- [ ] Review and update minimum stays:
  - Standard: 2 nights minimum
  - Weekends: 2 nights minimum
  - World Cup match dates: 7 nights minimum

---

## Pricing Rules

| Period | Strategy |
|---|---|
| Standard | 90th percentile of market |
| Last-minute (<3 days) | Drop 15–20% to fill gap |
| Events / holidays | 90th percentile + 20% buffer |
| World Cup dates | 7-night min · top-of-market rate |
| Jun–Sep (low season) | Lower base rate, shorter minimums |
| Dec–Apr (peak) | Raise base rate, longer minimums |

---

## Monthly Pricing Audit

- [ ] Review last month's occupancy rate — target 60%+
- [ ] Review last month's ADR — compare to neighborhood benchmark
- [ ] Review last month's RevPAR
- [ ] Check AirDNA data for market trends
- [ ] Adjust seasonal pricing if needed

---

## Tools

- **PriceLabs:** Dynamic pricing — syncs with Airbnb calendar
- **AirDNA:** Market data — $39.95/mo or $287.95/yr
- **Inside Airbnb:** Free comp data — insideairbnb.com
- **Miami tool:** Address Analyzer + Neighborhood Guide for benchmarks
