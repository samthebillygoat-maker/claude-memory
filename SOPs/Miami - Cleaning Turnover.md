# 🧹 SOP — Cleaning Turnover

**Standard turnover time:** 3–4 hours for 1BR/1BA

---

## Immediately After Checkout

- [ ] Strip all bedding — wash in hot water
- [ ] Collect all towels — wash separately
- [ ] Check for damage or missing items — photograph anything unusual
- [ ] Note inventory: toiletries, paper goods, supplies

---

## Kitchen

- [ ] Wash all dishes, dry, put away
- [ ] Wipe countertops and backsplash
- [ ] Clean stovetop and oven exterior
- [ ] Clean microwave inside and out
- [ ] Wipe refrigerator (exterior and interior shelves)
- [ ] Empty and reline trash bin
- [ ] Sweep and mop floor
- [ ] Restock: dish soap, sponge, paper towels

---

## Bathroom

- [ ] Scrub toilet (inside and out)
- [ ] Scrub shower/tub and tiles
- [ ] Clean mirror
- [ ] Wipe sink and faucet
- [ ] Wipe all surfaces
- [ ] Mop floor
- [ ] Restock: toilet paper (2 rolls), hand soap, shampoo, conditioner, body wash

---

## Bedroom

- [ ] Make bed with fresh linens — hotel fold on sheets
- [ ] Fluff and arrange pillows
- [ ] Wipe all surfaces (nightstands, dresser)
- [ ] Check under bed and in closet
- [ ] Vacuum carpet or sweep/mop hard floor

---

## Living Area

- [ ] Dust all surfaces
- [ ] Wipe TV screen (microfiber only)
- [ ] Straighten furniture
- [ ] Vacuum/sweep/mop
- [ ] Check that remotes have working batteries

---

## Final Check

- [ ] All lights working
- [ ] AC set to 74°F
- [ ] WiFi working
- [ ] Lockbox code active for next guest
- [ ] Take photos of clean unit (protects against damage claims)
- [ ] Mark unit ready in Airbnb
