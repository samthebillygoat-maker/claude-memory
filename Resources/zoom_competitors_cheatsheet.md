# Friday Pack vs The Big REI Lead Tools — Zoom Cheat-Sheet

_Last updated 2026-05-14. Pricing pulled live from each vendor + 3rd-party reviews._

---

## 1. PropStream — the 800-lb gorilla

- **What it is:** Nationwide property-data platform; the default "I need a list" tool for REI.
- **Pricing (2026):** Essentials ~$99/mo. Pro tier higher. Skip trace ~$0.12-$0.15/record. List Automator ~$27/mo add-on.
- **Data source:** Tax records + MLS + public records (static snapshots).
- **Does WELL:** Massive database, slick filters, distress flags (pre-foreclosure, tax delinquent, absentee), heat maps, rehab calculator. Brand recognition — every coach mentions it.
- **Doesn't do (vs Friday Pack):**
  - Zero active rental-listing signal — it's owner-of-record, not "this unit is listed today."
  - Phone numbers are skip-traced LLC contacts, not the leasing decision-maker who picks up.
  - No STR-friendliness filter at all. You'd manually cross-ref zoning.
  - DIY search — you build the list every week. Friday Pack is delivered pre-curated.

---

## 2. BatchLeads (+ BatchSkipTracing)

- **What it is:** All-in-one wholesaler stack — lists, skip trace, dialer, SMS, direct mail.
- **Pricing (2026):** Growth $71/mo, Professional $209/mo, Scale $449/mo. Skip trace bundled. BatchSkipTracing standalone is pay-per-match (~$0.10-$0.15/record), no subscription.
- **Data source:** Tax records + skip-trace databases + AI lead scoring (BatchRankAI).
- **Does WELL:** Best-in-class skip tracing, built-in dialer + SMS, BatchRankAI scoring is genuinely useful for wholesalers, integrated workflow under one login.
- **Doesn't do (vs Friday Pack):**
  - Built for off-market wholesale, not rental arbitrage — no listing-feed view.
  - Phone is skip-traced owner cell, not the person actually leasing the unit this week.
  - No STR-zoning or 12-month-lease filter.
  - You drive every search. No weekly curated drop.

---

## 3. DealMachine

- **What it is:** Driving-for-dollars app — pin houses on a map while you drive, then mail/call them.
- **Pricing (2026):** Starter $49/mo (1 driver, 500 traces), Pro $99/mo, Elite $249/mo. Skip trace $0.12-$0.14. Mail $0.72/piece. AI dialer $0.06/min.
- **Data source:** Driver-captured (photos) + tax records + skip trace.
- **Does WELL:** Best mobile UX in the category, AI dialer, on-the-ground vacant/distressed identification, cheap entry tier.
- **Doesn't do (vs Friday Pack):**
  - 100% off-market SFH flip/wholesale workflow — wrong universe for STR arbitrage.
  - No rental listings, period. You're hunting vacants, not leasable units.
  - Owner phone = LLC skip trace, not leasing agent.
  - No STR filtering, no curation — you literally drive the streets yourself.

---

## 4. REIPro

- **What it is:** Combined CRM + lead-gen + deal analyzer + contracts for solo investors.
- **Pricing (2026):** ~$57/mo single-user up to ~$197/mo; commonly cited at $109/mo. Mail and skip trace billed extra.
- **Data source:** Tax records + MLS + public records, similar to PropStream.
- **Does WELL:** End-to-end workflow (lead -> CRM -> contract -> close), good for buy-and-hold and flips, contract templates built in, 14-day free trial.
- **Doesn't do (vs Friday Pack):**
  - Same static property-record pool — no active rental-listings angle.
  - Owner contact, not landlord/leasing decision-maker who answers the phone today.
  - Zero STR-arbitrage lens — built for acquisition, not leasing.
  - DIY pull-your-own-list — no curated weekly delivery.

## Honest concessions (Sam, say these out loud)

- All 4 have better UI, deeper databases, and louder brand recognition than Friday Pack.
- PropStream/BatchLeads have 10x my engineering team (which is me).
- If the goal were buying houses off-market, I'd tell people to use PropStream, not me.
