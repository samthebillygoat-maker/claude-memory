# Miami-Dade STR Zoning — Per-ZIP Quick Reference

**Verified:** 14 May 2026 via municipal codes + Municode library.
**Use:** lead-pack credibility insert; pre-delivery filter before shipping
Miami leads to a paying customer or partner.

> Legend: ✅ STR-friendly · ⚠️ permit required (workable) · ❌ effective ban
> / unworkable for nightly arbitrage · 🟡 rules vary block-by-block

## Per-ZIP

- **33160 — Sunny Isles Beach** — ⚠️ Strict permit. STVR License + 13%
  bed-tax + **one-hour in-person responder for complaints**. Active
  enforcement scraping Airbnb/VRBO. Workable but expects inspection.
  Source: City of Sunny Isles Beach Code of Ordinances Ch. 217.
- **33180 — Aventura** — ⚠️ Permit required. Vacation Rental License +
  Certificate of Use + inspection. Civil penalty for operating without
  CofU: $100 first / $2,500 by 3rd offense in 24 months. Source: City of
  Aventura Code Compliance + Municode.
- **33154 — Surfside / Bal Harbour** — ❌ Unworkable for nightly arbitrage.
  Surfside **caps at 3 rental periods/12 months** ($100/period + BTR + 4%
  resort tax). Bal Harbour requires VRC under some of the strictest
  rules in South Florida. Fine for weekend-renter homeowners; kills
  weekly turnover. **Exclude from arbitrage packs.**
- **33141 — North Bay Village** — ⚠️ STVR program active. BTR + STVR
  registration. Posting requirements (occupancy, noise, 24-hr contact).
  Source: NBV Unified Land Dev. Code Ch. 13.
- **33161 — North Miami** — ❌ Effective ban. City code **prohibits
  rentals under 3 months** and prohibits **>4 leases in any 12-month
  period**. Advertising sub-3-month stays is also prohibited.
  **Exclude from arbitrage packs.**
- **33181 — North Miami Beach / Aventura edges** — 🟡 Varies. NMB
  jurisdiction caps at 3 STR periods/12 months. Aventura side =
  33180 rules. **Geocode each lead to the correct municipality
  before delivery.**
- **33172 — Doral east** — ⚠️ Certificate of Use. $136.17 initial /
  $36.70 annual renewal. Owner affidavit. Fines $500–$7,500 by 4th
  violation in 12 months. Workable.
- **33178 — Doral north/west** — ⚠️ Same as 33172 (Doral citywide).
  Western edges may fall under **Sec. 33-28 Miami-Dade County Code**
  (unincorporated) — county CofU + DBPR + DOR + liability insurance.

## Pack-shipping rules

- **Never include 33161 leads** without flagging the regulatory ceiling
  in the row.
- **Never include 33154 (Surfside) leads** in an arbitrage pack.
- **For 33181, annotate `municipality: NMB` vs `municipality: Aventura`**
  in the CSV so the operator knows which rule set applies.

## Operator-facing one-liner (for READMEs)

> "Of the 8 Miami-area ZIPs in this pack, two (33161, 33154) are
> effectively closed to nightly arbitrage operators under current
> municipal code. One (33181) is a coin-flip depending on the exact
> address. The other five (33160, 33180, 33141, 33172, 33178) are
> permit-required and workable, with Doral and Aventura being the
> most operator-friendly in terms of explicit licensing paths."
