# When to walk away

## Context
From [[Objections Live]].

## Content

If they've said "I need to think about it" twice, or if they're trying to negotiate below $300, or if they're asking you to do work outside scope (logo, business cards, etc), respond once kindly and let it go. Bad-fit clients eat 80% of your time for 20% of the revenue.

> "Totally fair — sounds like the timing isn't right. If anything changes, my number's the same. Take care."
