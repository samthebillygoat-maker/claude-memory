# When they get aggressive ("how did you get my number")

## Context
From [[Phone Scripts]].

## Content

Stay calm:
> Totally fair to ask. CSLB publishes contractor business phones publicly at cslb.ca.gov — I pulled the list of recently licensed roofers in California. I message each one once with a sample. If you don't want to hear from me again, just say so and I'll never contact you. Want me to remove you?

If yes, do it. Add to suppression list. Move on. Never re-engage.
