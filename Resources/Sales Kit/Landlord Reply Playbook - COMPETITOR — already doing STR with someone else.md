# COMPETITOR — already doing STR with someone else

## Context
From [[Landlord Reply Playbook]].

## Content

**Triggers:** "I already have an arbitrage tenant," "I'm working with another operator," "My unit is already on Airbnb."

**Subtext:** They are validating that the model works. They are also probably underpaid by the current operator.

**Response template:**
> Already with an operator?
>
> Smart — it sounds like you have already done the homework on whether this model works in {{city}}, which means I can skip the 101.
>
> Two questions: are they paying you on time every month, and is the rent indexed to what the unit could pull today versus what it pulled 18 months ago?
>
> If both answers are yes, you are in good hands and I will leave you alone. If either is a maybe, worth a 10-minute call to compare. Bad time to chat this week?
