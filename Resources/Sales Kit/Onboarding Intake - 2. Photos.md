# 2. Photos

## Context
From [[Onboarding Intake]].

## Content

Pick the option that's easiest:

- [ ] I'll send photos by reply email (5-15 photos of finished work, your crew, your trucks). JPG or PNG, any size.
- [ ] I'll send a Google Drive / Dropbox / iCloud link.
- [ ] No photos available — use stock photos relevant to my trade.
