# Onboarding Intake

# Customer Intake — {{CLIENT_BUSINESS}}

**Send this when a contractor says yes.** Reduces the email back-and-forth.
Reply with answers inline or attach a Word doc / PDF.

---

## 1. Confirmation

- Business name (exactly as you want it on the site): __________
- Tagline / slogan (1 sentence describing what you do): __________
- Cities you serve (list 4-6): __________
- Phone you want on the site: __________
- Email you want on the site: __________
- Years in business (for trust badges): __________
- CSLB # (just confirming): __________

## 2. Photos

Pick the option that's easiest:

- [ ] I'll send photos by reply email (5-15 photos of finished work, your crew, your trucks). JPG or PNG, any size.
- [ ] I'll send a Google Drive / Dropbox / iCloud link.
- [ ] No photos available — use stock photos relevant to my trade.

## 3. Services

List 4-6 specific services you want to feature on the site, in order of importance. Examples for reference (cross out what doesn't apply):

- Roof Replacement
- Roof Repair
- New Roof Installation
- Free Roof Inspections
- Storm Damage Claims
- Solar-Ready Roofing
- Tile / Metal / Shingle Roofing
- Insurance Claim Help

For each one, give me one sentence of what makes you different (e.g., "We finish a tear-off in 2 days, even on tile roofs").

## 4. Brand colors

- [ ] Match my truck/uniform — describe colors or attach a photo
- [ ] Match my logo — attach logo file
- [ ] Use what's in the mockup (we already picked palette)
- [ ] Specific colors: ____________ (hex codes welcome)

## 5. Reviews

- [ ] Pull live from my Google Business Profile (just need your Google Maps link)
- [ ] Pull from my Yelp page (Yelp URL: __________)
- [ ] I'll write 5 customer testimonials and send them
- [ ] Skip reviews section

## 6. Domain

- [ ] I already own a domain. Domain: __________ (registrar: GoDaddy / Namecheap / etc)
- [ ] I want to buy a new domain. Suggested: __________ (I'll buy and set it up, $10-20 cost)
- [ ] Use a free subdomain for now (yourname.pages.dev), buy a domain later.

## 7. Contact form (Tier 2/3 only)

When a visitor submits the contact form, where should the email go?

- Email: __________
- Subject prefix (e.g. "[Website Lead]"): __________

## 8. Special requests

Anything else — must-have features, things to avoid, deadlines (event coming up?), competitors I should look at?
