## 1. Scope

Designer agrees to design, develop, and deploy a website for Client at the following tier:

- [ ] Tier 1 — The Live Site ($300, 5 business days)
- [ ] Tier 2 — The Working Website ($750, 10 business days)
- [ ] Tier 3 — The Lead Engine ($1,500, 15 business days)

Deliverables for the selected tier are as described in PRICING.md (attached) and form part of this agreement.

## 2. Timeline

Project starts when Client pays the 50% deposit and provides any required content (photos, copy, brand colors). Timeline above is calculated from project start.

Designer commits to delivering on time. If Designer misses the timeline by more than 5 business days for reasons within Designer's control, Client receives a 10% refund per week of delay.

## 3. Payment

| Stage | Amount | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Deposit | 50% of tier price | Agreement signed |
| Final | 50% of tier price | Site live on Client's domain |

Stripe checkout links provided. No checks. Late final payment (>7 days post-launch) accrues $25/week.

## 4. Refund Window

Client may request a full refund of the $300 (Tier 1) within 7 days of launch for any reason. Tiers 2 and 3 are refundable up to and including the day before launch (50% deposit refund, less domain-purchase pass-through cost).

## 5. Revisions

Each tier includes the number of revision rounds listed in PRICING.md. Additional revisions are $75 each. "Revision" = up to 5 specific changes (e.g., "change the headline, swap the hero photo, recolor the CTA, fix the phone number, add a 4th service card").

## 6. Content & Photos

Client provides logo (if any), business photos, and any specific copy. If Client does not provide photos, Designer uses royalty-free stock relevant to the trade. Client owns all content they provide.

## 7. Hosting & Ownership

- Site is deployed on Cloudflare Pages under Client's domain.
- Domain is owned by Client; Designer may purchase on behalf if requested ($10-20 pass-through cost).
- After final payment, Client owns the website code and design. Designer may include the project in a portfolio (with name/photos blurred if Client requests).

## 8. Warranty

Designer warrants the site will function on current Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge for 90 days post-launch. Bugs reported in this window are fixed at no charge.

## 9. Limit of Liability

Designer's total liability is capped at fees actually paid by Client. Designer is not liable for downtime caused by domain registrars, hosting providers, or third-party services Designer doesn't operate.

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate before launch with written notice. Refund per Section 4. After launch, this agreement remains in effect through the warranty period (Section 8).

## 11. Communication

Primary channel: email to samthebillygoat@gmail.com. Designer responds within 1 business day during the project. Phone calls scheduled in advance via Calendly (link provided on signed agreement).

## 12. Independent Contractor

Designer is an independent contractor. No employment relationship is created. Designer is solely responsible for own taxes, insurance, and equipment.
