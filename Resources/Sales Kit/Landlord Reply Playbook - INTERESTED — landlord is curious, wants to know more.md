# INTERESTED — landlord is curious, wants to know more

## Context
From [[Landlord Reply Playbook]].

## Content

**Triggers:** "Tell me more," "How does this work?", "Sounds interesting," "What are the details?"

**Subtext:** They are leaning in but skeptical. They want to be reassured this is not a scam without you actually saying "this is not a scam."

**Response template:**
> Hey {{landlord_name}} — appreciate you coming back to me on {{address}}.
>
> Quick context so you can decide if this is worth a conversation: I run a small business that takes 12-month leases on properties like yours and uses them for furnished mid-term stays — corporate relocations, traveling nurses, insurance-displaced families. Every unit is fully insured, professionally cleaned between guests, and I am the named tenant on the lease.
>
> You are probably wondering who actually stays there, what happens if there is damage, and why I am not just renting traditionally. Happy to walk through all three on a 10-minute call.
>
> Is Wednesday or Thursday a bad time to connect?
