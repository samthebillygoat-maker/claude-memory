# Inbound: contractor calls you

## Context
From [[Phone Scripts]].

## Content

**They say:** "Hey, I got your text — I'm {name} from {biz}."

**You say:**
> Hey {name}, thanks for calling back. So I sent you a quick site mockup for {biz} — did it come through OK?

(They'll say yes or no.)

**If yes:**
> Cool. So I'm a 19-year-old college student teaching myself web design. I've been making sample sites for licensed CA contractors. The mockup I sent you took me about 20 minutes — what you'd actually get for $300 is exactly that, deployed live on your domain in 5 days. Does that feel useful to you?

**If no / didn't see it:**
> No worries — give me your email and I'll resend. While you're on the line — what's frustrating about your current site?

(Listen. Take notes. Their pain = your pitch.)
