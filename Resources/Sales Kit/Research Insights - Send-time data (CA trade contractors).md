# Send-time data (CA trade contractors)

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

Trade owners are in trucks 7am-3pm PT. They check phones at lunch and after dinner. Override the generic "Tue 10am" rule:

**Email:**
- Best: Tue / Wed / Thu **6:30-7:30am PT** (before they roll)
- Or: **7-9pm PT** (evenings — 6.52% reply rate per Belkins)
- Avoid: Fri afternoon, weekends, Mon mornings (overflow inbox)

**SMS / MMS:**
- Best: Tue / Wed / Thu **11:30am-1pm PT** (lunch break)
- Or: **5-7pm PT** (after work)
- TCPA hard cap: 8am-9pm recipient local time, only
