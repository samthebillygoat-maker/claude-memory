# HARD_NO — explicit rejection

## Context
From [[Landlord Reply Playbook]].

## Content

**Triggers:** "Not interested," "I don't do STR," "Take me off your list," "No subletting, period."

**Subtext:** They mean it. Do not push. But leave a door open — 30% of hard-nos reverse within 6 months when their tenant flakes.

**Response template:**
> Understood, {{landlord_name}}. Off the list.
>
> One last thing and then I am gone: it sounds like you have had subletting go sideways before, or you have a hard rule against it. Either is fair.
>
> If your situation on {{address}} ever changes — vacancy stretches, tenant breaks lease, market softens — my number is below. No follow-ups from me otherwise.
>
> Appreciate the straight answer.
