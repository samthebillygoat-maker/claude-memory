# Email format that wins

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

- Subject: 2-4 words, lowercase, question or first-name format
- Length: 75-100 words (51% response rate sweet spot per Artisan)
- Sentences: 6-8 max
- ONE call to action
- Problem-first positioning ("most contractor sites I see are missing X, costs you jobs")
- Reference a specific page/photo on their actual site in line 1

Length data: Lemlist found 120-word emails booked at 52% vs 20% for 300 words.
