# Outbound: you call them (after they reply YES via email)

## Context
From [[Phone Scripts]].

## Content

**Open with:**
> Hi {name}, this is Sam Lieberman — I sent you the site mockup for {biz} and you said you wanted to talk. Did I catch you at an OK time?

**If yes:**
> Cool. Three things to confirm fast: (1) you saw the mockup and like the direction, (2) you want it live on your own domain, (3) for Tier 1 it's $150 deposit + $150 on launch (50/50 split of the $300 total). Sound right?

**If they hesitate at any of those, just listen.** Don't sell over their concern. Address what they actually said.

**If they're ready:**
> Great. I'll send you the agreement and Stripe payment link in the next 10 minutes. Sign + pay the deposit, and I start tonight. Site goes live by {target_date}. Sound good?
