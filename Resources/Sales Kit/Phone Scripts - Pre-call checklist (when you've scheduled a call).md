# Pre-call checklist (when you've scheduled a call)

## Context
From [[Phone Scripts]].

## Content

- Open the contractor's mockup in a tab so you can refer to it
- Open `out/competitive_intel/{license}.md` so you know their site weaknesses
- Have Stripe Payment Links open and ready to paste
- Have `sales/SERVICE_AGREEMENT.md` ready to copy-fill
- Quiet room, headphones on, water within reach
- Smile while talking — they hear it
