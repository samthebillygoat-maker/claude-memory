# How to use

## Context
From [[Case Studies]].

## Content

When a reply comes in asking "have you done this?" or "what do other clients say?":

1. Pick the case study closest to their trade
2. Paste it into a reply (or attach as PDF — `pandoc CASE_STUDIES.md -o case_studies.pdf`)
3. Personalize the first line: "Funny you asked — here's a [trade] who was in a similar spot..."

Don't over-send. One case study per reply. Lead with the closest one.
