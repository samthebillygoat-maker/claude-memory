# Personalization layers (ranked)

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

The biggest reply-rate jumps come from showing you actually looked at the recipient. In order of leverage:
1. **Reference something specific from their actual site/photos** (5x lift)
2. **Use their first name in the body** (1.5x)
3. **Mention their city by name** (we already do this)
4. **Reference their license year** (we already do this)
5. **Generic AI-personalized greeting** ("Hi there" / "Hey friend") — actively hurts; sounds like spam
