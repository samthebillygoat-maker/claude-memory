# SOFT_NO — "let me think about it" / silence

## Context
From [[Landlord Reply Playbook]].

## Content

**Triggers:** "Let me think," "I'll get back to you," "Send me info and I'll review," then nothing.

**Subtext:** They are avoiding conflict. The real objection is unspoken.

**Response template:**
> Hey {{landlord_name}} — wanted to circle back on {{address}}.
>
> It seems like the timing or the structure isn't quite right, and I would rather know that than chase you.
>
> What would need to be true for this to be a no?
>
> If the answer is "nothing, it is a no," that is a totally fine response and I will stop reaching out.
