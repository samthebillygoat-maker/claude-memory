# TCPA compliance — non-negotiable

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

Cold SMS to a cell number without consent = $500-$1,500 per message in damages. CSLB business-line publication is NOT consent (Van Patten v. Vertical Fitness).

**Every SMS / MMS must include:**
- Business name (sender ID): "Sam, Sam's Web Design"
- Purpose statement (what is this about)
- Opt-out: `Reply STOP to opt out`
- Help: `HELP for info` (in initial message at minimum)
- Implied: "Msg & data rates may apply" (helpful, not always required)

**Quiet hours:** 8am-9pm recipient local. Stick to **9am-6pm weekdays** to be safe + actually convert.

**Honor STOP within 10 business days** (FCC April 2025 rule). Honor ANY reasonable opt-out phrasing ("leave me alone," "unsubscribe," "remove me"), not just "STOP." Twilio handles this automatically — don't disable.

**Records to keep 4 years (TCPA SOL):**
- Timestamp sent
- Recipient number
- Exact message body
- Consent basis (we're using "publicly listed CSLB business contact for B2B website outreach")
- Opt-out timestamp (if they reply STOP)
- Internal DNC list
