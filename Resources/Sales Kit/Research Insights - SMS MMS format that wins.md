# SMS / MMS format that wins

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

- **Use MMS with the mockup image** — 52% higher CTR than SMS-only (SimpleTexting)
- Cost is ~3x SMS but irrelevant at our volume
- The mockup is the entire pitch — show, don't link
- Pair with a single specific question ("worth a quick look?")
