# Pricing — Sam's Web Design

Three flat tiers. No hourly billing. No "consulting calls" first.

## Tier 1 — **The Live Site** — $300

What you get:
- Exactly the mockup I sent you, deployed live on a real domain
- 1 round of edits (colors / copy / photos)
- 1 contact form that emails you
- Deployed on Cloudflare Pages (free hosting forever)
- Domain transferred or purchased on your behalf

Timeline: 5 business days from go-ahead.

Best for: contractors who like the mockup and want to be online fast.

## Tier 2 — **The Working Website** — $750

Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Up to 5 pages (Home / About / Services / Projects / Contact)
- Photo gallery for past work (you send 10-15 photos, I edit + upload)
- Mobile-optimized for phone visitors
- Google Business Profile claim & link
- Basic SEO (meta tags, schema markup, fast load)
- 30 days of email support after launch
- 2 rounds of edits

Timeline: 10 business days.

Best for: contractors ready to use their site as a real lead source.

## Tier 3 — **The Lead Engine** — $1,500

Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Lead-capture form with auto-replies
- Estimate-request form with photo upload
- Live availability calendar (Calendly-style)
- Reviews scraper (pulls live Yelp/Google reviews onto site)
- Google Search Console + Analytics setup (you see who finds you)
- Local SEO content (blog posts targeting "[trade] in [city]")
- 60 days of email support
- 3 rounds of edits
- Optional $40/mo retainer after for ongoing edits & monitoring

Timeline: 15 business days.

Best for: contractors who want the site to actually generate calls.

## What's NOT included (any tier)

- Logo design (I'll use what you have, or a clean text logo)
- Photography (you provide photos OR I use stock relevant to your trade)
- Email hosting (use Gmail/Workspace separately, $6/mo)
- Paid ads / Google Ads management (separate engagement)

## Payment

50% to start, 50% on launch. Stripe checkout link sent with the agreement.
First $300 is fully refundable for **7 days from launch day** if you don't love the live site.

## How to start

Reply with which tier or just "Tier 1" / "Tier 2" / "Tier 3". I'll send the
agreement and a Stripe link. Site goes live on the timeline above.
