# What NOT to do

## Context
From [[Loom Playbook]].

## Content

- ❌ Don't read from a script — sound stiff, not human
- ❌ Don't trash their current site harshly. Diagnose, don't insult.
- ❌ Don't go past 90 seconds. Trade owners watch the first 30; if hooked, the next 60.
- ❌ Don't ask them to schedule a call in the video. The video IS the meeting.
- ❌ Don't include music or transitions. Plain screen recording.
