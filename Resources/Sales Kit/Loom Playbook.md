# Loom Playbook

# Loom Video Playbook

Research consensus: **a 60-90 second personalized Loom over their actual current website** is the single biggest reply-rate multiplier available (4% → 22% in field tests).

This is your "v2" playbook. Don't roll it out until v1 (text + inline mockup) has shipped 50+ emails so you have a baseline.

## Setup (5 min, free)

1. Sign up at https://loom.com — free tier gives you 25 5-minute videos/mo, plenty for indie volume.
2. Install the Loom Chrome extension.
3. Make sure your screen is clean (no other tabs visible) and your face is well-lit.

## Recording template (60-90 seconds)

**0:00-0:10 — Open with their name + something specific**
> "Hey {first_name} — I'm Sam, I'm 19 in California. I was looking at your site for {biz} and I noticed something..."

**0:10-0:35 — Walk through ONE specific weakness on their current site**
- Click through the homepage
- Resize browser to mobile width and show what breaks
- Try the contact form, try clicking the phone number
- "See how this is happening?" — point to the issue

**0:35-1:00 — Show your mockup as the fix**
- Open a tab with their mockup
- "Here's what it could look like instead — same business, just modernized."
- Scroll through key sections (hero, services, reviews, contact)

**1:00-1:20 — Soft CTA**
- "If you like it, I can build the real thing for $300 — 5 days, you own everything."
- "If not, no worries — just reply 'no' and I'll delete your file."
- "Either way, hope you have a good week."

## Filename + email pattern

After recording:
1. Loom auto-generates a thumbnail. Open the share settings → "Custom thumbnail" → download.
2. In email, embed the thumbnail as inline image, link it to the Loom URL.
3. Subject: `quick thing on {biz} site`

Email body (replace text version):
> Hey {first_name} —
>
> Made a quick 90-sec walkthrough of your current site + what a redesign could look like. ⬇️
>
> [thumbnail image of Loom, clickable to Loom URL]
>
> If you like it, $300 / 5 days for me to build the real thing. Reply 'yes' for the agreement.
> Not for you? Reply 'no' and I'll delete it.
>
> — Sam Lieberman, 19, web designer in CA

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't read from a script — sound stiff, not human
- ❌ Don't trash their current site harshly. Diagnose, don't insult.
- ❌ Don't go past 90 seconds. Trade owners watch the first 30; if hooked, the next 60.
- ❌ Don't ask them to schedule a call in the video. The video IS the meeting.
- ❌ Don't include music or transitions. Plain screen recording.

## Volume + pacing

- 1 Loom takes ~5 minutes to record + review
- 6 Looms = 30 min of focused work
- Send 6/day, Tue-Wed-Thu, 7pm PT (highest reply window for trades per research)
- Track in `replies` table: video sends should hit 12-22% reply vs 3-5% on text

## When to skip Loom and just send text

- If their existing site is already decent — Loom critiquing a good site looks petty
- If you've already sent them a text version (don't double-touch unless 14+ days passed)
- If you're sending more than ~10 in a day (Loom doesn't scale; switch to text-batch)
