# Outbound: voicemail when they don't answer

## Context
From [[Phone Scripts]].

## Content

> Hi {name}, this is Sam Lieberman — sent you the website mockup for {biz}. Just trying to connect for 5 minutes about whether it's useful. My number is {your_number}. Or just text me when you have a sec. Thanks.

(20 seconds. Don't pitch on voicemail. Just confirm who you are and offer a path back.)
