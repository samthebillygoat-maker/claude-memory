# The single highest-leverage change

## Context
From [[Research Insights]].

## Content

**Replace text-only emails with a 60-90 second personalized Loom recorded over their current website**, with a screenshot thumbnail embedded inline.

Why it stacks every multiplier:
- Visual personalization: 5x reply rate (3.4% → 18%)
- Video format: 4x reply rate in field tests (4% → 22%)
- Concrete proof-of-work over abstract claims

For a roofer who hates reading, watching you click through their broken mobile menu for 45 seconds is the single most persuasive artifact you can send.

**Subject line for this pattern:** "quick thing on {company} site" (lowercase, 4 words, references them specifically)
