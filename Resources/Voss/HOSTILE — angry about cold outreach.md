# HOSTILE — angry about cold outreach

**Triggers:** "How did you get my number?", "Stop spamming me," "This is harassment," profanity.

**Subtext:** Somebody else's cold call ruined their day, and you are the one who picked up the receipt. Do not match energy. Lower it.

**Response template (use FM DJ voice if on phone):**
> {{landlord_name}}, you are right to be annoyed. You posted a listing to find a tenant, not to get pitched.
>
> I got your contact off the public {{address}} listing — same place a normal tenant would. That does not make the interruption okay.
>
> I will take you off my list today. If you ever want to talk on your terms, my number is below. Otherwise you will not hear from me again.
