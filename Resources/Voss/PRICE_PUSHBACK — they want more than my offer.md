# PRICE_PUSHBACK — they want more than my offer

**Triggers:** "I need {{their_offer}}," "That's below market," "I can get more on the open market."

**Subtext:** They have an anchor in their head. They want to feel they negotiated, not capitulated.

**Response template (Ackerman, assumes target = {{my_offer}}):**
> {{their_offer}}?
>
> It sounds like you have a number in mind based on what comparable units are pulling in {{city}}. That makes sense.
>
> Here is my problem — at {{their_offer}}, the math does not work for me to take a 12-month commitment with zero vacancy risk to you. How am I supposed to make that work?
>
> What I can do is start at 65% of my ceiling and stretch from there. My honest opening is [65% non-round]. I have flexibility to move toward [85% non-round] if we can lock 12 months with a 30-day out clause for you, and I would cover the first professional deep-clean on move-out as a throw-in at [95% non-round]. The absolute top of what the unit pencils for me is {{my_offer}}.
>
> Where does that land for you?
