# ASKING_QUESTIONS — specific clarifying questions

**Triggers:** "Who actually stays there?", "What insurance do you carry?", "How do you handle damage?", "How often do guests turn over?"

**Subtext:** They are doing diligence. This is a buying signal disguised as a question. Answer crisply. Do not over-explain.

**Response template:**
> Good questions — let me take them in order.
>
> Guests: traveling nurses on 13-week contracts, corporate relocations, and insurance-displaced families. Average stay is 47 nights. No bachelor parties, no weekend rentals.
>
> Insurance: $2M commercial general liability, you are named as additional insured, and I carry a separate $50k contents policy on the furniture.
>
> Damage: I hold the security deposit you require on the lease. Beyond that, my own ops budget covers wear-and-tear so you are not getting nickel-and-dimed.
>
> You are probably also wondering what happens if I default. The 12-month lease means you are made whole regardless of my occupancy. Want to jump on a quick call to walk through the lease structure?
